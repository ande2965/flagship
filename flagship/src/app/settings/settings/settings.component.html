About Flagship

Flagship is an application supporting Star Wars Armada play though fleet building, analytics, and campaign management. It was created and is maintained by Joshua Barron. You can file issues or contribute to the development of Flagship at GitHub.

Privacy Policy

This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for Flagship. We only retain basic user information to provide persistence for created fleets and campaigns. We will never share your personal information with third parties except if required by law. Your continued use of our website will be regarded as acceptance of our practices around privacy and personal information. If you have any questions about how we handle user data and personal information, feel free to contact us on GitHub.
